The Problem

Dhaka is Drowning in Waste

Waste Dumping Site

5,000+ Tons Daily

Dhaka generates over 5,000 tons of waste daily, with most ending up in open dumps like Mugda.

Environmental Hazard

Rotting organic waste releases methane, pollutes groundwater, and creates breeding grounds for disease.

Wasted Resource

60% of this waste is organic and could become valuable compost instead of pollution.

Process

How BanglaKompost Works

1. Request Pickup

Fill out our simple form or call us. Tell us your location and waste type.

2. Local Hub Collection

Our team collects waste and brings it to the nearest local hub for sorting.

3. Smart Sorting

Non-biodegradable items are separated using ML-powered sorting (manual initially).

4. Composting Factory

Organic waste undergoes controlled decomposition at our composting facility.

5. Product Ready

Within 30-45 days, premium organic compost is ready for delivery.

About Us

Our Mission

BanglaKompost is on a mission to transform Bangladesh's organic waste problem into an opportunity for sustainable agriculture and cleaner cities.

We believe that every piece of organic waste has value. By collecting decomposable materials from households, markets, and restaurants, we create high-quality compost that enriches soil without harming the environment.

Our vision is to reduce waste in dumping sites like Mugda, provide affordable organic fertilizer to farmers, and create green employment opportunities across Bangladesh.
