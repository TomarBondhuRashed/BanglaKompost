BanglaKompost is an innovative waste management solution that collects organic waste from households, markets, and restaurants in Bangladesh, transforming it into valuable compost fertilizer within 30-45 days.

### Our Process

1. **Collect** - One-click/call waste pickup from your doorstep
2. **Process** - Smart sorting at local hubs (ML-powered in future)
3. **Compost** - Scientific composting at our facility
4. **Deliver** - Premium organic fertilizer to farmers
